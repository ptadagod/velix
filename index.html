Every visit opens on a full-bleed spotlight — title art, rating, genres and a one-line synopsis, with Play, watchlist and info right there. The backdrop crossfades between featured titles.

Rows that flow

Trending, Popular Movies, Popular Series and more — organized into clean, content-first rows that glide as you move. No clutter, no boxes fighting for attention. Just the art.

Pick up where you left off

Continue Watching remembers your spot across films and series — showing season, episode and exactly how much time is left, so the next play is always one click away.
